Start with the actual operational problem.

Do not lead with “we want AI.” Lead with what is breaking, slowing down, getting dropped, or creating unnecessary labor.

Good starting points

Serious buyers describe the business problem, not the trend they want to chase.

Strong starting point “We have too much manual follow-up and no clean visibility into the pipeline.”
Another strong starting point “Our systems do not talk to each other and work gets lost between handoffs.”
Bad starting point “We want something with AI because AI sounds modern.”
