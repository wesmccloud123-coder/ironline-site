About

Images that feel deliberate, not overworked.

Maya Lens creates portraits and brand imagery with a focus on human presence, strong contrast, and clean emotional direction.

I work with founders, agencies, artists, and publications that want photography with a point of view. My process is simple: reduce the noise, find the strongest visual tension in the frame, and make sure the final image still feels alive.

Whether the assignment is a studio session, a street-led editorial, or a brand campaign, the goal stays the same: create work people remember instantly.
