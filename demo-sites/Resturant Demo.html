Our Story

A chef’s table shaped by memory and craft.

Chef Marco Romano grew up in a family kitchen where meals lasted for hours and every recipe carried a story. After training in Rome and working in modern bistros across Europe, he opened Trattoria Roma to recreate that same feeling of generosity and ceremony.

The result is a restaurant built around handmade preparation, warm hospitality, and dishes that honor tradition while still feeling refined. Every plate is meant to slow the evening down.

“The best Italian meal doesn’t rush you. It invites you to stay.”
